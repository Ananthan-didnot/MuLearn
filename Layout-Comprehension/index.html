BATTLEGROUNDS MOBILE INDIA

India's Own Battle Royale

About BGMI

Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) is the Indian version of PUBG Mobile, specifically developed for the Indian market by Krafton. Launched in July 2021, BGMI quickly became one of the most popular battle royale games in India.

The game features intense battles where 100 players parachute onto an island, scavenge for weapons and equipment, and fight to be the last person or team standing while the play area continuously shrinks.

Key Features

Battle Royale

Classic 100-player survival mode across multiple maps with varying terrain and challenges.

Team Deathmatch

Fast-paced 4v4 combat in arena settings with respawns and weapon loadouts.

Arcade Modes

Quick matches including sniper training, war mode, and mini-zone for faster gameplay.

Esports Ready

Robust tournament systems and spectator modes for competitive play and streaming.

Popular Maps

Erangel

The classic 8x8 km map featuring diverse terrain including cities, farms, military base, and coastal areas.

Miramar

An arid, desert landscape with mountainous terrain, featuring cities, outposts, and unique structures.

Sanhok

A compact 4x4 jungle map with dense vegetation, rivers, and ancient ruins for fast-paced combat.

Pro Gameplay Tips

Master the Drop

Choose landing spots based on the flight path. Hot drops like Pochinki offer more action and better loot but higher risk, while remote locations allow safer looting.

Movement is Key

Never stand still, especially when looting. Use crouch and prone positions strategically, and utilize cover during firefights.

Sound Awareness

Use headphones to detect enemy movements. Sound cues are critical for locating opponents before they see you.

Vehicle Strategy

Vehicles offer rapid transportation but attract attention. Use them strategically and abandon them before approaching final circles.
